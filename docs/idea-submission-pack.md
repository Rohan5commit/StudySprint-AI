# Idea Submission Pack

## 1-line pitch
StudyPilot AI is a student web app that converts exam dates, weak chapters, free study hours, and learning style into a personalized revision system.

## Problem statement
Students often know they need to revise, but they do not know what to prioritize, how to break revision into steps, or how to turn raw notes into active study materials.

## Why this problem matters
Poor revision structure leads to wasted time, low confidence, and last-minute panic. This is especially real for school students juggling multiple subjects with limited study time.

## Target users
- secondary school students
- pre-university students
- first-year university students
- tutors who want faster revision prep

## Solution overview
StudyPilot AI asks for the student’s subject, chapter/topic, syllabus text, exam date, weak chapters, weekly free time, and preferred learning style. It then generates a complete revision system instead of a single AI answer.

## Core features
- personalized revision timetable
- chapter-by-chapter checklist
- quick summaries
- quizzes
- flashcards
- progress tracker
- last-minute revision mode

## Why this is realistic in an 8-hour final round
- Mostly frontend-led
- No heavy backend required
- Can run on local state with seeded demo data
- AI generation is limited to structured outputs, not complex orchestration
- Strong fallback mode makes the demo reliable

## What makes it innovative enough
The innovation is not a flashy model trick. It is turning AI into a full student workflow: prioritization, planning, active recall, progress tracking, and exam-pressure adaptation in one clean product.
