# Elevator Pitch

StudyPilot AI is a student study copilot that turns subjects, weak chapters, exam dates, and available study hours into a personalized revision system in minutes. Instead of making students manually create study plans, flashcards, and practice questions, the app generates a revision timetable, chapter checklist, summaries, quizzes, flashcards, progress tracking, and a last-minute revision mode. It is simple to understand, genuinely useful, and realistic to prototype fast for an AI-first buildathon.
